Privacy Policy for PickleNote

Effective Date: June 5, 2025

PickleNote ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and discloses information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. However, we use third-party services, such as Google AdMob, that may collect and use data as described below.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Data Collection

We use Google AdMob to display ads in our app. AdMob and its partners may collect and use information such as:

  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • IP-based location (approximate)
  • Ad interactions (views, clicks)
  • User interests (for personalized and behavioral ads)

This data may be used for tracking purposes as defined by Apple’s AppTrackingTransparency framework. Users may be prompted for permission to allow tracking, in compliance with iOS policies.

More about how Google uses data: Google Partner Sites Policy.

3. App Permissions and Features

PickleNote may request access to the following features:

  • Audio Playback: Plays short MP3 files stored locally. No audio recording or microphone usage occurs.
  • Storage/Network Access: Required to load app assets and serve ads.

We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or precise location.

4. Tracking and Privacy Controls

To comply with privacy regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA, and Apple's ATT), you may see a prompt requesting permission to track your activity across apps and websites for ad personalization.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has submitted personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share user data directly. However, data collected by Google AdMob may be shared with third parties for ad delivery and tracking, under their respective privacy policies.

7. Managing Your Privacy

You can manage or limit ad tracking via:

  • Device Settings > Privacy > Tracking (on iOS)
  • Google Ads Settings: https://adssettings.google.com
  • Limit ad personalization or reset ad ID on Android
